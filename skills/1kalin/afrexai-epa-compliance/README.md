# Environmental Compliance Manager

Full EPA and state environmental regulatory compliance for manufacturing, construction, energy, and logistics operations.

## What's Inside

- **6 federal program coverage**: Clean Air Act, Clean Water Act, RCRA, CERCLA, EPCRA, TSCA
- **Penalty exposure tables**: Know exactly what each violation costs ($25K-$75K/day)
- **Facility classification**: RCRA generator status, CAA source categories
- **Inspection readiness checklists**: Universal, hazardous waste, stormwater
- **Reporting calendar**: Every mandatory filing deadline mapped
- **Risk scoring framework**: Weighted compliance assessment with action tiers
- **Penalty mitigation**: 7 factors EPA uses to reduce fines, including voluntary disclosure

## Who This Is For

Any business that generates waste, has air emissions, handles chemicals, or discharges water. Manufacturing plants, construction sites, logistics hubs, energy facilities, chemical distributors, food processors.

Environmental violations are among the most expensive regulatory penalties in the US. One missed permit renewal or unreported release can cost more than your annual compliance budget.
