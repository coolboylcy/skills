## What This Skill Covers

- **FTC Funeral Rule** — GPL, CPL, itemized statements, embalming consent, third-party casket rules
- **State Licensing** — Director/embalmer requirements, CE hours, apprenticeship by state
- **Preneed Compliance** — Trust deposit requirements, insurance funding, Medicaid planning, annual reporting
- **Cremation Regulations** — Waiting periods, authorization, pacemaker removal, scattering laws
- **Green Burial** — GBC standards, biodegradable containers, conservation easements
- **Pricing Benchmarks** — NFDA median costs, itemized breakdowns, revenue per call targets
- **Operational KPIs** — 10 metrics every funeral home should track
- **OSHA** — Formaldehyde exposure, bloodborne pathogens for prep room staff
