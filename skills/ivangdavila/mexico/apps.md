# Useful Apps for Mexico Travel

## Transport and Navigation

- **Google Maps** — baseline routing and saved lists
- **Moovit** — useful public transit support in larger cities
- **Uber / Didi** — practical in many urban areas for night returns

## Booking and Dining

- **OpenTable and direct booking links** — useful for top restaurants
- **Airline and bus operator apps** — better disruption updates than aggregators

## Weather and Outdoors

- **Windy / weather apps** — useful for coastal and storm-aware planning
- **Offline maps** — essential backup for road routes and cenote or hike days

## Practical

- **Wise / Revolut-style apps** — payment and exchange tracking
- **Translation apps** — useful outside major tourism corridors

## Workflow Tip

Use discovery apps first, then confirm details directly with official operators before paying for high-value bookings.
