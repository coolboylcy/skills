## Emergency Numbers

- **Emergency services:** 911

## Health and Medical

- Travel insurance is strongly recommended for visitors.
- Keep passport copy, medication list, and emergency contacts offline.
- In major cities and tourism hubs, private clinics can be faster for non-critical needs.

## Transport and Area Safety

- Use planned transport after late nights.
- Avoid unnecessary late-night route improvisation in unfamiliar areas.
- Build arrival-day plans around known transfer options.

## Weather and Outdoor Safety

- Heat and humidity can raise risk quickly.
- Coastal weather windows can change plans with short notice.
- Carry water, power backup, and offline maps on outdoor days.

## When to Escalate

- Call 911 for immediate danger, injury, fire, or urgent police/medical support.
- For non-urgent cases, use hotel or host support to find appropriate local services.
