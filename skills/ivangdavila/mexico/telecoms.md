## Main Options

- eSIM plans are practical for short stays.
- Local prepaid SIM can be better for longer stays or hotspot-heavy needs.
- Coverage is strongest in cities and varies in remote beach or mountain areas.

## Typical Traveler Strategy

1. Compare eSIM first for short urban trips.
2. For longer or multi-region trips, evaluate prepaid local plans.
3. Check planned-route coverage before committing.

## Practical Notes

- Telcel, AT&T Mexico, and Movistar are common choices.
- Urban coverage is usually strong; remote routes can be uneven.
- Keep offline maps for road, cenote, and hiking days.

## What to Check Before Purchase

- Data cap and throttling rules
- Hotspot allowance
- Coverage in planned regions
- Recharge and support options
