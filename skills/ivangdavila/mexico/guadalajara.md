# Guadalajara Complete Guide

## City Pattern

### Americana and Lafayette
**Vibe:** Cafes, bars, design shops, modern city rhythm.
**Best for:** Food and nightlife-first stays.

### Centro Historico and Cultural Axis
**Vibe:** Historic buildings, plazas, museums, daytime focus.
**Best for:** Architecture and classic city context.

### Tlaquepaque (day/half-day)
**Vibe:** Artisan district, galleries, restaurants.
**Best for:** Slower cultural pace and shopping.

## Guadalajara and Jalisco Add-on

- Tequila town routes are best as one dedicated day with transport planned in advance.
- Weekend demand rises quickly in Americana dining and bar zones.
- Keep one flexible slot for weather, traffic, or late-night recovery.

## Mistakes to Avoid

1. Overpacking city center plus Tequila route in one day.
2. Ignoring reservation windows in high-demand restaurants.
3. Assuming all nightlife clusters have the same safety and transport profile.
