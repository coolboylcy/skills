# Toronto International Cuisine Guide

## Chinese

### Cantonese / Hong Kong Style
Toronto has arguably the best Cantonese food outside Hong Kong.

**Dim Sum**
- **Dynasty Centre** — Chinatown classic
- **Crown Princess** — Yorkdale area
- **Rol San** — Chinatown, late night

**BBQ / Roast Meats**
- **King's Noodle** — Chinatown institution
- **Swatow** — Chinatown
- **Rol San** — Multiple locations

**Seafood**
- **Lai Wah Heen** — Fine dining, downtown
- **Fishman Lobster** — Markham

**Areas**: Chinatown (Spadina), Markham, Scarborough (Agincourt)

### Sichuan / Hunan
Spicy, numbing, bold.
- **Szechuan Legend** — Chinatown
- **Peaceful Restaurant** — Sichuan classics
- **Sichuan Garden** — North York

### Shanghainese
Soup dumplings (xiao long bao), noodles.
- **Mother's Dumplings** — Multiple locations
- **Dumpling House** — Yorkville area

### Northern Chinese / Dongbei
Hearty, wheat-based.
- **Zhang's** — Steele/Yonge
- **Magic Oven** — Handpulled noodles
