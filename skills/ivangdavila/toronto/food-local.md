# Toronto Local & Canadian Food

## Canadian Classics

### Poutine
Quebec origin, now everywhere in Toronto.

**The Traditional**
- French fries, cheese curds, gravy
- Curds should squeak
- **Best Traditional**: Poutini's House of Poutine

**Variations Toronto Does Well**
- Butter Chicken Poutine — fusion classic
- Korean Poutine — bulgogi, kimchi
- Lobster Poutine — Maritime-inspired
- **Best Loaded**: Smoke's Poutinerie (chain but good)

### Peameal Bacon
Toronto's signature food. Canadian bacon rolled in cornmeal.

**The Classic Sandwich**
- Peameal on a kaiser bun
- Optional: mustard, cheese, egg
- **The Original**: Carousel Bakery, St. Lawrence Market
- **Other Options**: St. Urbain Bagel, Rashers

### Butter Tarts
Canadian pastry icon. Flaky crust, gooey filling.

**The Debate**: Runny vs. firm, raisins vs. no raisins

**Best In Toronto**:
- Phipps Bakery
- Tori's Bakeshop (vegan option)
- Circles and Squares

### Nanaimo Bars
BC origin, layered chocolate/custard bar.
- Available at most bakeries
- Best at: Dufflet Pastries

### Montreal-Style Bagels
Smaller, sweeter, wood-fired. Different from NY bagels.

**Best In Toronto**:
- St. Urbain Bagel (Eglinton)
- Kettlemans (Ottawa style)
- Bagel House

### Montreal Smoked Meat
Similar to pastrami, different spices.
- **Best**: Caplansky's legacy spots
- **Jewish Delis**: Centre Street Deli, Pancer's

## Toronto Signatures

### Toronto-Style Pizza
Unique style: square cut, sometimes sweet sauce, Canadian toppings.
- Not fine dining, but nostalgic
- **Try**: Pizza Nova, Bitondo's

### Portuguese Chicken
Toronto's Portuguese community influence.
- Charcoal grilled, piri-piri sauce
- **Best**: Churrasqueira do Sardinha, Piri Piri Grillhouse
- **Area**: Dundas West (Little Portugal)

### Jamaican Patties
Golden pastry, spiced meat filling.
- Ubiquitous in Toronto
- **Best**: Randy's Take Out, Patty King
- **Chain**: Patty Palace, Warden station legend

### Hakka Chinese
Unique Toronto thing: Indian-Chinese fusion from Hakka Chinese who lived in India.
- Chili chicken, manchurian
- **Best**: Hakka Legend, Federick, Lin Garden

### Toronto Island Lemonade
Fresh-squeezed at Centre Island during summer.
- Part of the Toronto Islands experience
- Only available on islands
