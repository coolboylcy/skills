# Dublin Complete Guide

## Neighborhoods — Where to Go

### South City Core (Grafton/Stephen's Green)
**Vibe:** Walkable center, shopping, museums, easy first-day orientation.
**Best for:** First-time visitors, compact sightseeing loop.

### The Liberties
**Vibe:** Historic and local, better value than tourist-heavy core blocks.
**Best for:** Distillery visits, traditional pubs, evening atmosphere.

### Smithfield + Stoneybatter
**Vibe:** Local-food growth zone, strong pub and cafe mix.
**Best for:** Nights out without central tourist markup.

### Docklands
**Vibe:** Modern Dublin, business and newer dining options.
**Best for:** Contemporary hotels and cleaner late-evening walks.

## City Movement

- Walking covers most center highlights.
- LUAS (tram) is efficient for cross-city hops.
- Taxis are easy but surge during event nights.

## Mistakes to Avoid

1. Spending every night only in Temple Bar.
2. Packing too many ticketed sites in one day.
3. Ignoring reservation needs for prime dinner slots.
4. Assuming pub kitchens run late everywhere.
