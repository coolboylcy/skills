## Social Norms

- Friendly conversation is common in pubs, shops, and taxis.
- Queue etiquette is expected in transport and service settings.
- Humor and understatement are common in local communication.

## Pub Culture

- Pub atmosphere varies from tourist-heavy to highly local.
- Traditional music sessions are often informal but venue-dependent.
- Respect space and volume in smaller local pubs.

## Practical Etiquette

- Be polite with service staff and drivers, especially during peak pressure times.
- Confirm opening hours; schedules can differ by town and season.
- In rural areas, patience and slower pace expectations help.

## Budget and Payment Notes

- Card payment is common almost everywhere.
- Tipping is generally more moderate than in US settings.
- Peak-event weekends can shift prices quickly.
