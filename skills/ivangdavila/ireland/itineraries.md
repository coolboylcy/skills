# Ireland Itineraries

## 5 Days — Dublin + One Coast Extension

### Days 1-2 Dublin
- City walk, museum, pub and food night split

### Days 3-5 Galway or Cork base
- One scenic day + one local culture day + one flexible half-day

**Best for:** First-time short trip without fatigue overload.

## 7 Days — Dublin + Galway + Clare/Kerry sample

### Days 1-2 Dublin
### Days 3-4 Galway
### Days 5-7 Clare or Kerry focus

**Best for:** Mixed city + scenery plan with moderate driving.

## 10 Days — Balanced Ireland Route

### Days 1-3 Dublin
### Days 4-5 Cork/Kinsale
### Days 6-7 Kerry route section
### Days 8-10 Galway/Connemara

**Best for:** Travelers wanting variety without full-island overreach.

## Seasonal Notes

- Peak summer: lock stays and key activities early.
- Shoulder months: better value and easier reservations.
- Winter: stronger for city culture, weaker for long scenic driving density.
