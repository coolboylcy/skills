## Core Rule

Pick hikes by current conditions, footwear readiness, and time budget, not just social-media visuals.

## Strong Areas

### Wicklow (near Dublin)
- Great for day-hike access from city stays.

### Connemara and west routes
- Rugged scenery and variable conditions.

### Kerry trails
- Classic route options with strong scenic payoff.

## Safety Basics

- Rain gear and layers are essential year-round.
- Keep offline map backups.
- Start earlier than expected for longer routes.
- Turn back when visibility or footing conditions degrade.

## Mistakes to Avoid

1. Casual shoes on muddy or rocky routes.
2. Ignoring changing weather in open terrain.
3. Starting too late and racing daylight.
4. Treating all posted route times as universal.
