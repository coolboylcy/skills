## Emergency Numbers

- **Emergency services:** 112 or 999

## Health and Medical

- Travel insurance is strongly recommended for visitors.
- Keep key documents, medication list, and emergency contacts offline.

## Road and Weather Safety

- Coastal winds and rain can change road confidence quickly.
- Slow down on narrow rural roads and blind bends.
- Build weather fallback into all scenic-day plans.

## Night Safety Basics

- Use licensed transport after late nights.
- Stay aware in high-density nightlife streets.
- Keep backup payment and phone battery buffer.

## When to Escalate

- Call 112/999 for immediate danger, injury, fire, or urgent police help.
- Use local non-emergency channels for lower-priority issues.
