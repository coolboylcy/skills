## Main Options

- Major carrier coverage is strong in cities and weaker in remote coast sections.

## Traveler Strategy

1. Compare eSIM for short stays first.
2. For longer trips, evaluate prepaid local plans with hotspot allowance.
3. Check rural coverage before committing to one provider.

## Practical Notes

- Urban coverage is usually strong.
- Coastal and remote segments can have weaker signals.
- Keep offline maps for rural driving and hiking days.
