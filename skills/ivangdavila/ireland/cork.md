# Cork Complete Guide

## City and Surroundings

### Cork City Core
**Vibe:** Compact, food-forward, relaxed pace.
**Best for:** Market visits, pub dining, short city base.

### Kinsale (day/overnight)
**Vibe:** Harbor town, polished food scene.
**Best for:** Seafood-focused stops and slower evenings.

### West Cork Extensions
**Vibe:** Scenic villages, coastal drives, artisan food.
**Best for:** Travelers who prioritize pace and landscape over checklist volume.

## Cork Strategy

- Use Cork as 2-3 night base for city + coast split.
- Do not overfill with too many long same-day loops.
- Weekends can book out early in peak season.

## Mistakes to Avoid

1. Treating Cork only as a pass-through stop.
2. Overcommitting to long drives after late nights.
3. Expecting every small-town restaurant to take walk-ins in summer.
