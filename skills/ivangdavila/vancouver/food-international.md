# International & Fine Dining Vancouver

## Fine Dining

### Top Tier ($200+/person)

**Hawksworth**
- **Location**: Hotel Georgia, Downtown
- **Style**: Contemporary Canadian, elegant
- **Known for**: Power dining, celebrations, tasting menus
- **Price**: $150-250/person
- **Book ahead**: 2-3 weeks
- **Note**: Vancouver's most prestigious address for special occasions

**St. Lawrence**
- **Location**: Railtown
- **Style**: French-Canadian, Quebec-inspired
- **Known for**: Tourtière, foie gras, nose-to-tail
- **Price**: $150-200/person
- **Book ahead**: 2 weeks
- **Note**: Critics' darling, brings Montreal to Vancouver

**Botanist**
- **Location**: Fairmont Pacific Rim
- **Style**: Pacific Northwest, garden-inspired
- **Known for**: Cocktail program, views, modern
- **Price**: $150-200/person
- **Book ahead**: 1-2 weeks

**Tojo's**
- **Location**: West Side
- **Style**: Omakase Japanese
- **Known for**: Invented California roll, legendary chef
- **Price**: $200-400/person
- **Book ahead**: 1-2 weeks
- **Note**: Sit at chef's bar if possible

### High-End ($$$ | $80-150/person)

**Boulevard Kitchen & Oyster Bar**
- **Style**: Seafood, raw bar, elegant
- **Known for**: Oysters, seafood tower
- **Book ahead**: 1 week

**Chambar**
- **Style**: Belgian-Moroccan fusion
- **Known for**: Moules frites, brunch, space

**Blue Water Cafe**
- **Style**: Seafood, sushi, raw bar
- **Known for**: Best seafood in city, sablefish
- **Book ahead**: 1-2 weeks weekend

**L'Abattoir**
- **Location**: Gastown

- **Book ahead**: 1 week

**AnnaLena**
- **Location**: Kitsilano
