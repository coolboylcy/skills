# Asian Cuisines in Vancouver

## Chinese

### Dim Sum Capital
Vancouver has the best dim sum in North America. Period.

**Top Picks:**
| Restaurant | Area | Style | Price | Notes |
|------------|------|-------|-------|-------|
| **Dynasty Seafood** | Richmond | Classic Cantonese | $$ | Locals' favorite |
| **Kirin** | Multiple | Upscale | $$$ | Beautiful presentations |
| **Sun Sui Wah** | Main St | Traditional | $$ | Roast meat excellence |
| **Chef Tony** | Richmond | HK-style | $$ | Modern touches |
| **Pink Pearl** | East Van | Old school | $$ | Institution since 1981 |

**Dim Sum Strategy:**
- Go on weekdays to avoid lines
- 10-11am sweet spot
- Push carts = traditional experience
- Order sheet = fresher food
- Classic must-orders: har gow (shrimp), siu mai (pork), cheung fun (rice rolls), char siu bao (BBQ pork buns)

### Regional Chinese

**Cantonese (most common):**
- Dynasty, Sun Sui Wah, Kirin
- Roast meats: HK BBQ Master, Parker Place
- Seafood: Sea Harbour, Neptune

**Sichuan:**
- **Alvin Garden**: Numbing authentic heat
- **Chong Qing**: Late-night favorite
- **Legendary Noodle**: Hand-pulled, spicy
- Order: mapo tofu, dan dan noodles, water-boiled fish

**Northern/Xi'an:**
- **Xi'an Cuisine**: Hand-pulled noodles, lamb
- **Wang's Shanghai**: Soup dumplings (xiao long bao)
- **Peaceful Restaurant**: Xinjiang-style lamb

**Hong Kong-style:**
- **Congee Noodle House**: Comfort food, late night
- **Cafe Gloucester**: Milk tea, French toast
- **Kam Wai Dim Sum**: Bakery-style quick dim sum

### Chinese Food Courts
Best-kept secret for authentic, cheap eats:
- **Crystal Mall** (Burnaby): Massive, diverse
- **Aberdeen Centre** (Richmond): High-quality food court
- **Parker Place** (Richmond): More local, less tourist
- **Yaohan Centre** (Richmond): Japanese + Chinese

## Korean

### Korean BBQ
| Restaurant | Area | Style | Notes |
|------------|------|-------|-------|
| **Sura** | West End | Upscale | Beautiful banchan |
| **Samsoonie** | Multiple | Classic | Value |
| **Kook** | Multiple | Modern | Trendy |
| **Insadong** | Robson | Traditional | Late night |

**Korean BBQ tips:**
- Usually order per person (meat portioned)
- Unlimited banchan (side dishes)
- Lettuce wraps are the move
- Soju and Korean beer available

### Korean Fried Chicken
The craze hit Vancouver hard:
- **Zabu Chicken**: Pioneer, multiple locations
- **Chick Chick**: Extra crispy
- **Pelicana**: Chain, consistent

### Korean Comfort
- **Sushi Maro**: Surprisingly good Korean (kimbap, budae jjigae)
- **Ma Dang Goul**: KBBQ + comfort food
