# Seoul Neighborhoods — Quick Comparison

## Overview Map

Seoul is divided into 25 districts (구, "gu"). Key areas for expats and visitors:

| Area | Vibe | Best For | Metro Access |
|------|------|----------|--------------|
| **Gangnam** | Business, modern, wealthy | Professionals, K-pop fans | Excellent |
| **Seocho** | Upscale residential, quiet | Families, professionals | Good |
| **Hongdae** | Youth, nightlife, creative | Young people, nightlife | Excellent |
| **Itaewon** | International, diverse | Expats, LGBTQ+, foodies | Good |
| **Hannam** | Upscale expat, quiet | International families | Moderate |
| **Yongsan** | Developing, central | Tech workers, central access | Excellent |
| **Jongno** | Traditional, historic | Culture lovers, tourists | Excellent |
| **Mapo** | Hip, creative | Young professionals | Good |
| **Songpa** | Family, modern | Korean families, Lotte Tower | Good |
| **Pangyo** | Tech hub | Tech workers | Moderate |
