# Seoul Itineraries

## 1-Day Itinerary (Highlights Sprint)

### Morning (9 AM - 12 PM)
**Gyeongbokgung Palace Area**
1. Arrive at Gyeongbokgung (Line 3, Gyeongbokgung Station Exit 5)
2. Rent hanbok nearby (₩15,000-25,000) for free palace entry
3. Watch guard changing ceremony (10 AM)
4. Walk to Bukchon Hanok Village (15 min walk)
5. Wander traditional alleyways, photos

### Lunch (12 PM - 1:30 PM)
**Insadong**
- Walk south to Insadong (10 min)
- Try: Traditional Korean lunch (bibimbap, samgyetang)
- Browse: Tea houses, craft shops

### Afternoon (1:30 PM - 5 PM)
**Namsan Tower**
- Take taxi or bus to Namsan cable car station
- Cable car up (₩11,000 round trip)
- Explore observation deck
- Walk down through the park (40 min) or cable car

### Evening (5 PM - 10 PM)
**Myeongdong → Hongdae**
1. Myeongdong for street food dinner (tteokbokki, hotteok)
2. K-beauty shopping if interested
3. Metro to Hongdae (Line 2, 20 min)
4. Night scene: bars, clubs, street performers

## 3-Day Itinerary (Essential Seoul)

### Day 1: Traditional Seoul

**Morning**
- Gyeongbokgung Palace + Bukchon (see 1-day)
- National Folk Museum (inside palace grounds, free)

**Lunch**
- Tosokchon Samgyetang near palace (famous ginseng chicken soup)

**Afternoon**
- Walk through Samcheong-dong (cafes, galleries)
- Changdeokgung Palace + Secret Garden (book tour in advance)

**Evening**
- Gwangjang Market for dinner (bindaetteok, mayak kimbap, soju)
- Cheonggyecheon Stream night walk

### Day 2: Modern Seoul

**Morning**
- COEX Mall + Starfield Library (opens 10:30 AM)
- Gangnam neighborhood walk

**Lunch**
- Korean BBQ in Gangnam (any 고기집)

**Afternoon**
- Lotte World Tower observation deck (book sunset slot)
- Seokchon Lake walk (next to tower)

**Evening**
- Jamsil for dinner options
- Banpo Bridge Rainbow Fountain (April-October, 7:30 PM shows)
- Or: Itaewon for international food + bars

### Day 3: Hip Seoul + Day Trip Option

**Option A: Full DMZ Day**
- Book full-day DMZ tour (USO or Koridoor)
- Returns around 4-5 PM
- Evening: Hongdae for farewell dinner + nightlife

**Option B: Art + Culture**

**Morning**
- Leeum Museum (Samsung art museum)
- Hannam-dong boutiques and cafes

**Lunch**
- Itaewon for international cuisine

**Afternoon**
- War Memorial of Korea (free, 2-3 hours)
- Yongsan Electronics Market (if interested)

**Evening**
- Hongdae street performances (start 6-7 PM)
- Dinner: Korean fried chicken + beer
- Nightlife: clubs, karaoke (노래방)

## 7-Day Itinerary (Complete Experience)

Follow 3-day itinerary above

- Express Bus Terminal Underground Shopping

**Full Day**

**Evening**
- Naksan Park sunset views

## Practical Notes

### Getting Around
- Buy T-money card at any convenience store (₩4,000 card + load money)
- Download Naver Maps or KakaoMap (Google Maps doesn't work well in Korea)
- Metro runs 5:30 AM - midnight

### Booking Required
- DMZ tours: Book 3-5 days ahead
- Changdeokgung Secret Garden: Book same day online, limited spots
- Popular restaurants: Reserve via Naver/Kakao or call

### Money Tips
- Most places take cards, but keep ₩50,000-100,000 cash
- ATMs: Look for "Global ATM" signs (7-Eleven, CU)
- No tipping anywhere

### Weather Adjustments
- Summer: Indoor activities midday, outdoor morning/evening
- Winter: Layer up, activities still accessible
- Monsoon (July): Have indoor backup plans
