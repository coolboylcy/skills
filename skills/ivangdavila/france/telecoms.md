## Main Options

- eSIM plans are practical for short stays.
- Local prepaid SIM can be better for longer trips and hotspot usage.
- Coverage is strongest in cities and varies in remote mountain or rural routes.

## Typical Traveler Strategy

1. Compare eSIM first for short city-led trips.
2. For longer multi-region travel, evaluate prepaid local plans.
3. Check coverage maps for planned routes.

## Practical Notes

- Major carriers and budget brands offer broad urban coverage.
- Rural valleys and mountain routes can have weaker signal windows.
- Keep offline maps for hikes and village driving days.

## What to Check Before Purchase

- Data cap and throttling rules
- Hotspot allowance
- Coverage in planned regions
- EU roaming terms if crossing borders
