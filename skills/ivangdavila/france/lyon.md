# Lyon Complete Guide

## City Pattern

### Presqu'ile
**Vibe:** Central shopping, classic squares, easy city flow.
**Best for:** First-time orientation and short city stays.

### Vieux Lyon
**Vibe:** Old-town lanes, traditional bouchon atmosphere.
**Best for:** Heritage walks and classic local dining.

### Croix-Rousse
**Vibe:** More local rhythm, cafes, creative energy.
**Best for:** Slower mornings and neighborhood-based meals.

## Lyon Strategy

- Use Lyon as 2-3 night base for food and day-trip flexibility.
- Lunch menus often outperform dinner value.
- Book bouchons in advance on peak weekends.

## Mistakes to Avoid

1. Treating Lyon only as a transfer stop.
2. Booking no-reservation dining on busy weekends.
3. Overplanning too many day trips from one short stay.
