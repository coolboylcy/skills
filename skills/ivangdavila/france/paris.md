# Paris Complete Guide

## Neighborhoods — Where to Go

### Le Marais
**Vibe:** Historic streets, boutiques, strong cafe and dining mix.
**Best for:** First-time stays with walkable culture and food options.

### Saint-Germain and Left Bank
**Vibe:** Classic Paris identity, museums, polished dining.
**Best for:** Museum-heavy and architecture-focused plans.

### Canal Saint-Martin and Oberkampf
**Vibe:** Local nightlife and younger restaurant scene.
**Best for:** Better value dinners and evening bar routes.

### Montmartre edges
**Vibe:** Iconic hill views with high tourist flow.
**Best for:** Early starts and short scenic windows.

## City Movement

- Metro and RER handle most routes efficiently.
- Walking between close neighborhoods often beats taxi in dense zones.
- Museum reservations save major queue time.

## Mistakes to Avoid

1. Eating every meal near the biggest landmarks.
2. Overstacking major museums in one day.
3. Assuming top restaurants always accept walk-ins.
4. Ignoring transit strikes or disruption checks.
