# France Itineraries

## 6 Days — Paris + One Extension

### Days 1-3 Paris
- Museum and neighborhood split days
- One food-focused route with reservations

### Days 4-6 Lyon or Marseille
- One city culture day
- One food and nightlife day
- One flexible half-day before transfer

**Best for:** First-time trip with low transfer fatigue.

## 9 Days — Paris + Lyon + Provence/Riviera Lite

### Days 1-3 Paris
### Days 4-6 Lyon
### Days 7-9 Nice or Marseille base

**Best for:** Travelers wanting city and coast contrast.

## 12 Days — Balanced Multi-Region Route

### Days 1-4 Paris
### Days 5-7 Lyon
### Days 8-10 Provence base
### Days 11-12 Riviera finish or Paris return buffer

**Best for:** Mixed travelers who can handle more transfer complexity.

## Seasonal Notes

- Peak summer needs earlier accommodation and transport booking.
- Shoulder months usually improve value and reservation access.
- Winter favors city culture and food, with shorter daylight.
